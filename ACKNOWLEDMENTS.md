# Acknowledgments

AbstractVision stands on the shoulders of excellent open-source projects and communities.

## Upstream projects

- **Hugging Face Diffusers** (model pipelines and utilities used by the local Diffusers backend): `src/abstractvision/backends/huggingface_diffusers.py`
- **PyTorch** (local inference runtime used by Diffusers)
- **stable-diffusion.cpp** (local GGUF runtime used by the stable-diffusion.cpp backend): `src/abstractvision/backends/stable_diffusion_cpp.py`
- **stable-diffusion-cpp-python** (pip-installable python bindings used as a fallback runtime)

## Community and contributors

Thanks to everyone who reports issues, suggests improvements, and contributes fixes or documentation updates.
